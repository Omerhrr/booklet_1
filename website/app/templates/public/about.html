About Booklet

Empowering businesses with smart, simple, and powerful management tools.

Our Story

Booklet was born from a simple idea: business management software should be accessible, affordable, and actually useful. We saw too many small and medium businesses struggling with complex, expensive tools that required extensive training.

Founded in 2024, we set out to build a platform that combines powerful accounting, inventory management, CRM, and business intelligence tools in one intuitive interface. Our mission is to help businesses focus on what they do best while we handle the numbers.
